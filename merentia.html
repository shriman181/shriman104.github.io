Merentia comes from a simple conviction: the mid-market specialty chemical world is running twenty-year-old R&D cycles while a handful of giants pull ahead with materials AI. That gap isn't a technology problem — the science exists. It's a distribution problem, and closing it is the whole company.

"Find the signal, learn the mechanism, build the system, put skin in the game. Formulation is just the newest place to run that loop."

Shriman builds at the intersection of messy operational data and AI — turning the kind of information that normally lives in a senior chemist's head and a decade of batch records into models a manufacturer can actually deploy.
